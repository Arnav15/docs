YugaByte DB’s storage and replication architecture is similar to that of [Google Cloud Spanner](cloud.google.com/spanner/) which is also a CP database with high write availability. While Google Cloud Spanner leverages Google’s proprietary (and expensive) network infrastructure, YugaByte DB is designed work on commodity infrastructure used by most enterprise users.

A post on our blog titled [Practical Tradeoffs in Google Cloud Spanner, Azure Cosmos DB and YugaByte DB](https://medium.com/p/practical-tradeoffs-in-google-cloud-spanner-azure-cosmos-db-and-yugabyte-db-ce720e07c0fd) goes through some of above topics in more detail.
